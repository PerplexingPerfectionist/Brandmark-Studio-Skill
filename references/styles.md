# Style Engine

## Style Families

### Modern
- **Minimalist**: Maximum reduction, essential forms only, generous whitespace, geometric precision. Low complexity, high negative space, uniform line weight, zero ornamentation.
- **Geometric**: Pure shapes (circle, square, triangle), grid-based construction, mathematical ratios. Medium complexity, strong negative space, consistent line weight, no organic curves.
- **Swiss**: Grid systems, asymmetric balance, functional typography, objective clarity. Medium complexity, structured negative space, bold weight contrasts, zero decoration.
- **Contemporary**: Current trends, fluid geometry, soft gradients, human-centered. Medium complexity, balanced negative space, variable line weight, minimal ornamentation.
- **Startup**: Friendly geometry, approachable proportions, bold color, slightly playful. Medium complexity, moderate negative space, medium-bold weight, minimal ornamentation.
- **Corporate**: Stable, established, authoritative, understated. Low complexity, conservative negative space, uniform weight, zero ornamentation.

### Classic
- **Vintage**: Aged textures, period typography, warm palettes, nostalgic resonance. Medium-high complexity, moderate negative space, variable weight, moderate ornamentation.
- **Heritage**: Tradition, legacy, trustworthiness, timeless forms. Medium complexity, balanced negative space, medium weight, subtle ornamentation.
- **Art Deco**: Geometric luxury, stepped forms, sunburst motifs, gilded elegance. Medium-high complexity, decorative negative space, bold weight, strong ornamentation.
- **Emblem**: Shield forms, contained marks, institutional authority, heraldic structure. Medium complexity, enclosed negative space, medium-bold weight, moderate ornamentation.
- **Badge**: Circular or crest forms, ribbon elements, award-like quality. Medium complexity, enclosed negative space, medium weight, moderate ornamentation.

### Luxury
- **Premium**: Refined minimalism, generous spacing, restrained palette, quality signaled through restraint. Low complexity, abundant negative space, thin to medium weight, zero ornamentation.
- **Editorial**: Sophisticated, cultured, magazine-quality, typographic confidence. Low-medium complexity, generous negative space, thin weight, minimal ornamentation.
- **Monogram**: Interwoven letterforms, elegant ligatures, personal mark, signature quality. Medium complexity, dense negative space within letters, variable weight, subtle ornamentation.
- **Boutique**: Hand-crafted feel, artisanal quality, exclusive, limited edition. Medium complexity, moderate negative space, variable weight, subtle ornamentation.

### Creative
- **Illustrative**: Narrative imagery, detailed scenes, character-driven, story-rich. High complexity, complex negative space, variable weight, strong ornamentation.
- **Hand Drawn**: Imperfect lines, organic irregularity, human touch, authentic. Medium-high complexity, organic negative space, variable weight, natural ornamentation.
- **Sketch**: Rough, exploratory, unfinished quality, process-visible. Medium-high complexity, loose negative space, light weight, minimal ornamentation.
- **Whimsical**: Playful, unexpected, imaginative, lighthearted. Medium complexity, playful negative space, variable weight, moderate ornamentation.
- **Playful**: Bold, energetic, colorful, approachable fun. Medium complexity, dynamic negative space, bold weight, moderate ornamentation.

### Entertainment

- **Esports**: Aggressive, angular, high contrast, team identity. High complexity, sharp negative space, heavy weight, strong ornamentation.
- **Mascot**: Character-based, anthropomorphic, relatable, personality-forward. High complexity, organic negative space, variable weight, strong ornamentation.
- **Streaming**: Live energy, modern, connected, content-forward. Medium-high complexity, dynamic negative space, medium-bold weight, moderate ornamentation.

## Style Blending

Blend characteristics rather than choosing one style. When blending:

1. **Identify dominant style**: One style should lead (60-70%).
2. **Borrow secondary traits**: Extract 2-3 defining characteristics from the secondary style.
3. **Resolve conflicts**: If styles contradict, favor the dominant style's core principles.
4. **Test at small sizes**: Blended styles risk complexity; verify the mark holds at 16×16.

### Common Successful Blends
- **Gothic + Minimalist**: Dark mood through silhouette, minimal ornamentation. Preserve Gothic geometry (pointed arches, dramatic proportions) but strip decorative tracery. High negative space, bold silhouette, zero texture.
- **Art Deco + Cyberpunk**: Geometric luxury meets neon futurism. Preserve Art Deco stepped forms and symmetry, add Cyberpunk neon glow and glitch effects. High contrast, symmetrical, digital ornamentation.
- **Organic + Luxury**: Natural forms with refined restraint. Preserve organic curves and botanical motifs, render with Luxury precision and generous spacing. Abundant negative space, thin to medium weight, minimal ornamentation.
- **Nordic + Tech**: Functional warmth meets digital precision. Preserve Nordic generosity and natural materials, add Tech grid alignment and pixel clarity. Generous negative space, medium weight, minimal ornamentation.
- **Japanese + Minimalist**: Asymmetric minimalism. Preserve Japanese asymmetry and brushwork quality, apply Minimalist reduction. High negative space, variable weight, zero ornamentation.
- **Vintage + Modern**: Nostalgic core with current clarity. Preserve Vintage warmth and period proportions, apply Modern geometric precision and clean paths. Moderate complexity, balanced negative space, medium weight, minimal ornamentation.
