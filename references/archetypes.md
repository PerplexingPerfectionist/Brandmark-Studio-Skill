# Archetype System

## Supported Archetypes

Innocent, Explorer, Sage, Hero, Outlaw, Magician, Creator, Ruler, Caregiver, Everyman, Lover, Jester.

Archetypes influence: symbolism, shape language, composition, emotional tone, visual metaphors.

---

## Innocent

- **Emotional goal**: Safety, purity, optimism, trust
- **Visual tendencies**: Soft curves, rounded forms, light colors, open spaces, simple shapes
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Circles, clouds, flowers, stars, hearts, soft animals, gentle landscapes
- **Shape language**: Round, organic, soft, unthreatening
- **Composition**: Centered, balanced, generous padding, no sharp edges
- **Recommended for**: Wellness, children's products, food, sustainability, non-profits

## Explorer

- **Emotional goal**: Freedom, discovery, authenticity, adventure
- **Visual tendencies**: Irregular shapes, diagonal lines, compass motifs, maps, arrows, rough textures
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Mountains, compasses, arrows, paths, globes, footprints, wild animals
- **Shape language**: Asymmetrical, angular, dynamic, open-ended
- **Composition**: Off-center, diagonal energy, leading lines suggesting journey
- **Recommended for**: Travel, outdoor gear, automotive, education, research, adventure brands

## Sage

- **Emotional goal**: Wisdom, truth, knowledge, clarity
- **Visual tendencies**: Clean geometry, book/scroll motifs, owl-like forms, structured grids, balanced proportions
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Books, eyes, owls, trees, lamps, keys, scrolls, scales, geometric precision
- **Shape language**: Balanced, proportional, measured, intellectual
- **Composition**: Symmetrical or near-symmetrical, centered focal point, clear hierarchy
- **Recommended for**: Education, consulting, technology, publishing, research, analytics

## Hero

- **Emotional goal**: Courage, mastery, achievement, protection
- **Visual tendencies**: Strong verticals, bold forms, shield motifs, upward motion, powerful silhouettes
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Shields, swords, crowns, stars, lions, eagles, wings, upward arrows, mountains
- **Shape language**: Strong, upright, bold, triangular or shield-like
- **Composition**: Vertical emphasis, centered power, bold silhouette, no weakness in form
- **Recommended for**: Fitness, security, sports, leadership, military, emergency services

## Outlaw

- **Emotional goal**: Rebellion, revolution, freedom, disruption
- **Visual tendencies**: Broken symmetry, sharp angles, distressed textures, unconventional forms, high contrast
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Skulls, lightning, broken chains, daggers, flames, anarchic symbols, torn edges
- **Shape language**: Irregular, jagged, asymmetrical, rebellious, aggressive
- **Composition**: Unbalanced, tilted, aggressive angles, unexpected proportions
- **Recommended for**: Gaming, motorcycles, alternative music, streetwear, edgy tech, counterculture

## Magician

- **Emotional goal**: Transformation, wonder, vision, mystery
- **Visual tendencies**: Fluid forms, spirals, eye motifs, starbursts, impossible geometry, glowing effects
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Wands, stars, eyes, crystals, moons, lightning, spirals, portals, mystical symbols
- **Shape language**: Flowing, transforming, mysterious, unexpected
- **Composition**: Centered but dynamic, radiating energy, illusion of depth, transformation visible
- **Recommended for**: Entertainment, technology, creative agencies, wellness, spiritual, luxury

## Creator

- **Emotional goal**: Innovation, imagination, self-expression, originality
- **Visual tendencies**: Abstract forms, brushstrokes, geometric experimentation, mixed media feel, color-forward
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Brushes, pencils, lightbulbs, DNA strands, atoms, paint splatters, geometric abstractions
- **Shape language**: Experimental, expressive, abstract, original
- **Composition**: Asymmetrical, unexpected balance, creative negative space, layered elements
- **Recommended for**: Art, design, technology, startups, education, media, creative tools

## Ruler

- **Emotional goal**: Control, power, leadership, excellence
- **Visual tendencies**: Strong horizontal and vertical lines, crown motifs, geometric precision, authoritative weight
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Crowns, thrones, lions, eagles, scepters, geometric seals, laurels, architectural columns
- **Shape language**: Authoritative, stable, commanding, architectural
- **Composition**: Centered, stable, dominant, heavy at base or top, clear hierarchy
- **Recommended for**: Finance, luxury, government, enterprise, consulting, premium services

## Caregiver

- **Emotional goal**: Compassion, nurture, service, protection
- **Visual tendencies**: Soft curves, embracing forms, hand motifs, heart shapes, warm colors, gentle geometry
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Hands, hearts, trees, shields, umbrellas, medical symbols, animals with young, embracing figures
- **Shape language**: Open, protective, rounded, nurturing, non-threatening
- **Composition**: Centered, soft, open forms that suggest protection or embrace, warm proportions
- **Recommended for**: Healthcare, insurance, non-profits, education, family, social services

## Everyman

- **Emotional goal**: Belonging, realism, equality, down-to-earth
- **Visual tendencies**: Simple, approachable forms, no pretension, honest geometry, functional design
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Basic tools, everyday objects, simple geometric shapes, human silhouettes, houses, roads
- **Shape language**: Simple, honest, functional, unpretentious
- **Composition**: Balanced, straightforward, no dramatic angles, clear and direct
- **Recommended for**: Retail, food, home goods, automotive, basic services, mass market

## Lover

- **Emotional goal**: Intimacy, sensuality, passion, connection
- **Visual tendencies**: Curves, flowing lines, intertwined forms, heart motifs, warm palettes, elegant proportions
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Hearts, intertwined figures, roses, lips, candles, rings, infinity symbols, flowing ribbons
- **Shape language**: Curved, flowing, sensual, elegant, connected
- **Composition**: Flowing, intertwined elements, warm proportions, generous curves, elegant balance
- **Recommended for**: Beauty, fashion, romance, hospitality, luxury, relationships, lifestyle

## Jester

- **Emotional goal**: Joy, humor, playfulness, spontaneity
- **Visual tendencies**: Unexpected shapes, playful proportions, bright colors, asymmetrical energy, whimsical forms
- **Symbolic tendencies**: Masks, hats, stars, confetti, playful animals, exaggerated features, smile motifs
- **Shape language**: Playful, exaggerated, unexpected, dynamic, humorous
- **Composition**: Off-balance, unexpected angles, playful proportions, surprising negative space
- **Recommended for**: Entertainment, food, children's products, gaming, social media, casual brands
